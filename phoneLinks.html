phoneLinks

Users expect to click on a phone number and be able ot call the person right away.
New standards allows web developers to put this right in thier html. 
<!--Example One-->
<a href="tel:1-800-555-5555">(800) 555-5555</a>
<!--Example Two-->
<a href="tel:1-800-555-5555">Call me!</a>
One most smart phones, this will prompt the user is they would like to make the call (showing them the number they are about to call, to protect them from scams or expensive out of country calls).

Make sure your phone number is large enough to be clicked! This means it shouldn’t be much smaller than the size of your finger.

I’ve also had some issues with pesky line breaks when dealing with phone numbers. One way around this is to use none breaking spaces and hyphens.
<a href="tel:1-800-555-5555">(800)&nbsp;555&#8209;5555</a>
